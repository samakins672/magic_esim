FAQs about eSIM cards from Magic eSIM

What is an eSIM and how can you use Magic eSim?
eSIM, or embedded SIM (also known as a digital SIM card, electronic SIM card, or virtual SIM card), is a cloud-based mobile number that enables users to activate and manage multiple cellular plans on one device. It removes the need for a physical SIM card and provides the convenience of having several cellular providers on a single device. eSIM also offers extra features, such as the ability to add a secondary line for international travel and separate data plans for personal and business use.
Can I make phone calls or send SMS messages?
Our prepaid eSIM plans are designed for data-only use and do not include a mobile phone number. For voice calls and text messages, we recommend using VoIP apps like Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp, Viber, Telegram, or Skype. This allows users to stay connected globally without the need for a traditional international SIM card from mobile operators or carriers when traveling.
Can an eSIM be reused?
Each QR code is a unique identifier that can only be used on one device. It cannot be reused on another device or the same device after the eSIM profile is deleted. This ensures the code remains secure and is only used on the intended device.
When does the eSIM mobile data plan start?
The eSIM data plan duration begins immediately after the purchase is completed. If you don’t plan to use mobile data right away, you can select "Activate later" during the purchase of a new prepaid plan and activate it within the next 12 months.
Can I extend the expiration of my current eSIM cellular data plan?
You can easily extend your plan. We'll notify you via push notification and email when you've used 70% of your data, so you can top up your Magic eSim account anytime through our app. The app offers various payment options, making it simple to choose the best one for you.
Is eSIM secure?
Yes, eSIM technology is secure for several reasons. First, eSIMs are embedded directly into devices, making it hard for unauthorized users to remove or tamper with them. Second, eSIMs use secure elements to store sensitive data like your mobile identity, helping prevent hacking. Additionally, activating an eSIM requires scanning a QR code or entering a specific activation code, adding an extra layer of security. Overall, eSIM technology offers a strong and reliable way to secure your mobile communications.
Can I use tethering or a Personal Hotspot?
You can easily share mobile data with other devices using your Magic eSim profile. Simply install the Magic eSim profile on your device and enable the Personal Hotspot in the settings. This feature is enabled by default, and there are no extra charges for using it. However, any data used while tethering will be deducted from your plan.
How do I transfer my eSIM to a new phone?
Before transferring your eSIM, ensure that your new smartphone supports eSIM technology. Contact our team to have a new eSIM profile created, then log into your Magic eSim account on the new device to install the QR code. Make sure to confirm that you are using a different device to ensure the settings are updated correctly.
Are there any contracts like traditional eSIM carriers?
You don’t need to sign any contract to use Magic eSim’s eSIM. You can activate and deactivate it at any time based on your travel needs.
Why is my data speed slow?
The speed of your mobile data can be influenced by various factors, including the type of smartphone you're using, the network technology available, the websites or apps you're accessing, the time of day, network congestion, and the data usage in your area. Additionally, download and upload speeds can depend on the network infrastructure and broadband connection type in your country. Slow internet speed is often caused by poor coverage from the mobile operator and can usually be improved by restarting your phone.
What makes Magic eSim different from other eSIM services?
Magic eSim stands out from other eSIM providers by partnering with local operators in over 200 destinations worldwide, offering affordable data packs without intermediaries, ensuring strong coverage, providing virtual phone numbers, integrating VPN technology for added security, and offering a seamless activation process.
